## v2.1 - 2024-12-19

**Refinement** | Status changed to "evolving"

Thesis status changed from "active" to "evolving" as conflicting signals accumulated. Disinflation in goods offset by persistent services inflation. The structural thesis remains intact but timeline and magnitude require revision.

**Triggering Signals:**

- Services inflation remaining sticky
- Fed rate cut timing debates
